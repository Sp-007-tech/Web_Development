HTML Entity Example

  1. The less-than sign: <

  2. The greater-than sign: >

  3. The ampersand (and) sign: &

  4. The double quote sign: "

  5. The single quote sign: '

  6. The cent sign: ¢

  7. The pound sign: £

  8. The yen sign: ¥

  9. The euro sign: €

  10. The copyright sign: ©

  11. The registered trade mark sign: ®
